We have all been hearing about the amazing plans to renovate the courts at Golden Gate Park Tennis Center. It is a big project, many years in the making and made possible by the hard work of dozens of dedicated tennis players. As we get close to the finish line on fundraising for the project, there will be a big party on Saturday, September 8th to officially launch the fundraising campaign.

This free event will feature fun and engaging on-court activities, exhibition matches from local college and junior players and a live screening of the US Open Women's Final on a 14 x 18 big screen. There will also be food trucks and a wine and beer garden, as well as games for kids and prizes for all.

We'll be there and hope that you can join us as the community kicks off the "final set" of fundraising for this amazing project. And even if you can't be there on the 8th, you can support the cause by donating to the campaign. Your gift will be doubled by Taube Philanthropies, which is generously matching every dollar raised - up to $6.5 million.
